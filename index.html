Introduction

Over the past three decades, Wendy Schamaun has combined advanced education, military service, and mission-driven leadership to support global high-threat and risk assessment efforts for the Department of Defense, Department of State, and the United Nations. She holds a master's degree with honors in Emergency and Disaster Management.

In the U.S. Navy, Wendy was one of the first two women to graduate and serve under the Naval Protective Services designator, directly supporting NCIS.

Her career has taken her across the globe, where she built long-term relationships with top-performing operators and service members, all focused on one mission: safeguarding people and communities.

With nearly 50 FEMA certifications, including instructor, medical, incident command, CBRNE, and high-threat global security, Wendy and her extended operational network are built to train, consult, mentor and manage teams in high-consequence environments.
